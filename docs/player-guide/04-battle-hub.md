# The Battle Hub

The Battle Hub is your live dashboard during a tournament. Everything you need — your current match, pairings, standings, and match history — is accessible from one screen.

![Battle Hub overview](./04-battle-hub.png)

## Tabs

The Battle Hub has four tabs. Which ones are visible depends on the tournament state and whether you're checked in.

| Tab | When it appears | What it shows |
|-----|-----------------|---------------|
| **Match** | When you're checked in and a round is active or in scoring | Your current matchup, table number, and scoring interface |
| **Pairings** | Always (once pairings are posted) | All matchups for the selected round — every player, table, and result |
| **Standings** | After the first round completes | The live leaderboard with rank, W-L-D record, and tournament points |
| **History** | Always | Your past matches across all completed rounds |

## Round Selector

At the top of the hub, a **round selector** lets you navigate between rounds. Use the dropdown or arrow buttons to jump to any round. The current round is selected by default.

When viewing a past round, the Pairings tab shows that round's matchups and results. The Standings tab always shows the latest cumulative standings.

## Round States

Rounds progress through several states during the tournament:

| State | What's happening |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Pending** | Round hasn't started yet — no pairings posted |
| **Locked** | Pairings are set but the round hasn't started. You can see your table and opponent, plus the scheduled start time |
| **Active** | Round is in progress — play your game |
| **Scoring** | Round has ended — time to enter your scores |
| **Complete** | All scores submitted and confirmed |

## Stats Strip

After the first round completes, a stats strip appears below the check-in card showing your running totals: wins, losses, draws, and total tournament points. This updates after each round.

## Tournament Complete

When the event is over, the Battle Hub shows a completion screen with final standings and your finishing position.
