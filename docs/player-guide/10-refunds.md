# Refunds & Cancellations

If you've registered for a paid tournament and can no longer attend, you may be able to request a refund directly from OWR — provided you're within the refund window.

![Refund card](./10-refunds.png)

## Refund Eligibility

A refund is available when all of these conditions are met:

- The tournament has a **paid entry fee** processed through OWR (Stripe)
- Your **payment has been completed** (not still pending)
- You haven't already been refunded
- The current date is **before the refund deadline** set by the tournament organiser

If you're eligible, a **Refund** card appears at the bottom of your registration confirmation page.

## Requesting a Refund

1. Go to **My Events** and click on the tournament
2. On the confirmation page, scroll to the **Refund** section at the bottom
3. Click the **Request Refund** button
4. Confirm the refund request

The refund is processed through Stripe. Your payment is reversed and your registration state changes to `Refunded`. This frees up a spot for other players (or the next person on the waitlist).

## Refund Deadline

Tournament organisers set a refund deadline — typically a few days before the event. After this date, the refund option disappears and you'll need to contact the TO directly if you need to cancel.

The refund deadline is determined by the organiser and may vary between events.

## Offline Payments

If you paid outside of OWR (cash, bank transfer, etc.), the refund system doesn't apply. Contact the tournament organiser directly to arrange a refund.

## After a Refund

Once refunded:
- Your registration state shows as **Refunded**
- You're removed from the attendee list
- If the tournament had a waitlist, the next person in line may be promoted to a confirmed spot
- The refund amount appears on your original payment method (processing time depends on your bank)
