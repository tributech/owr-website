# Standings & Match History

As the tournament progresses, the Battle Hub keeps you updated with live standings and a history of all your matches.

![Standings tab](./09-standings-history.png)

## Standings Tab

The **Standings** tab appears after the first round completes. It shows the live leaderboard for the tournament.

Each row in the standings table includes:

| Column | What it shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Rank** | Your position in the tournament |
| **Player** | Player name |
| **Faction** | The army faction they're playing |
| **W-L-D** | Win, Loss, Draw record |
| **TP** | Total Tournament Points |

### Highlights

- **Your position** is highlighted so you can spot yourself at a glance
- The **top 3** positions have a subtle background highlight
- Rankings update after each round completes

## Stats Strip

After the first round, a stats strip appears at the top of the Battle Hub showing your personal running totals — wins, losses, draws, and total tournament points. This updates in real time as matches are completed.

## History Tab

The **History** tab shows all your past matches across every completed round. Each entry is displayed as a card showing:

- **Round number**
- **Opponent name and faction**
- **Outcome** — Win, Loss, or Draw with colour coding (green/red/yellow)
- **Score** — VP or TP depending on the scoring mode
- **"In Progress"** indicator — if the match is in the current active round

You can also click through to view the current round's match from the History tab.

## Tournament Complete

When the final round is complete and the TO finalises results, the Battle Hub shows a tournament completion screen with:

- Your **final rank** and finishing position
- The complete **final standings** table
- Your **total tournament points** and W-L-D record

Results are then reflected in your player profile and regional rankings.
