# Check In on Event Day

When you arrive at the tournament venue, head to the **Battle Hub** to check in. The Battle Hub is your home screen for the entire event — it's where you'll see pairings, enter scores, and track standings.

![Check-in card](./03-check-in.png)

## Getting to the Battle Hub

Navigate to the tournament page and click **Battle Hub**, or go directly to the URL your TO has shared. You'll need to be logged in with the account you registered with.

## The Check-In Card

At the top of the Battle Hub you'll see the **Player Event Status** card. This shows:

- **Event Day** — "Day 1 of 2" for multi-day events, or "Today" for single-day events
- **Your Name & Faction** — Confirms who you're registered as
- **Army List Status** — A colour-coded badge:
  - **List Approved** (green) — Your list has been reviewed and approved
  - **List Submitted** (amber) — Submitted but not yet reviewed
  - **List Required** (red) — You haven't submitted a list yet
- **Registration Status** — A badge in the top-right corner showing your current state (Confirmed, Checked In, etc.)

## Checking In

When check-in is open, a green **Check In** button appears on the card. Tap it and your status changes from `Confirmed` to `Checked In`. That's it — you're ready to play.

If check-in hasn't opened yet, you'll see a message: "Check-in opens on event day".

## Multi-Day Events

For multi-day tournaments, check-in may be required each day. The card updates to show the current day number and you'll need to check in again at the start of each day.

## After Check-In

Once checked in, the **Match** tab becomes available in the Battle Hub (if the round is active). You'll see your current match, table assignment, and opponent details. Head to your table and get playing.
