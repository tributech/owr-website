# Find & Register for a Tournament

OWR lists every upcoming Warhammer: The Old World tournament in your region. Once you've found an event you'd like to attend, registering takes just a few clicks.

![Registration page](./01-find-register.png)

## Finding a Tournament

There are a few ways to discover events:

- **Upcoming Events** — The main events page shows all upcoming tournaments in your region, sorted by date.
- **Search** — Use the search bar in the top nav to find tournaments by name.
- **Region Selector** — Switch regions using the dropdown to browse events in other countries.

Each tournament listing shows the key details at a glance: name, dates, venue, player cap, army points, number of rounds, and entry fee.

## Registering

From the tournament page, click the **Register** button. You'll see a registration screen showing the tournament details — dates, venue, rounds, points, player cap, and entry fee.

### Requirements

Before you can register, you need:

1. **An OWR account** — If you're not logged in, you'll see **Sign In** and **Create Account** buttons. After signing in you'll be redirected straight back to the registration page.
2. **A player profile** — If you don't have one yet, you'll be prompted to **Create Player Profile**. This links your account to a player record for rankings and results.

Once both are in place, you'll see a "Registering as" card with your name and regional rank.

### Free Events

For free tournaments, click the **Register** button. You'll be asked to confirm — click **Confirm Registration** and you're in.

### Paid Events (Stripe)

If the event has an entry fee and accepts payments through OWR, click **Register & Pay**. You'll be taken to a Stripe checkout page to complete payment. Once payment succeeds, your registration is confirmed automatically.

### Paid Events (Offline Payment)

Some tournament organisers handle payments outside of OWR. In that case you'll see a **Register** button with a confirmation dialog that shows the entry fee and notes that payment details will be provided by the organiser.

### Full Tournaments & Waitlist

If all spots are taken, you can still **Join Waitlist**. You'll be placed in a queue and notified if a spot opens up. Your waitlist position is shown on the confirmation page.

## Confirmation Page

After registering, you'll land on the confirmation page. This shows:

| Section | What it shows |
|---------|---------------|
| **Status Header** | Your registration state — Confirmed, Waitlisted, or Payment Pending |
| **Waitlist Position** | Your place in the queue (if waitlisted) |
| **Payment Card** | A **Pay Now** button if payment is still required |
| **Tournament Card** | Event summary with dates, venue, and your faction |
| **Army List Card** | Submit or update your army list (see next guide) |
| **Next Steps** | Reminders — submit your army list by the deadline, check in on event day |
| **Refund Card** | Request a refund if you're within the refund window |

From here you can also navigate to **View My Events** (your dashboard) or **View Tournament** (the public event page).
